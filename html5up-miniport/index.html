Andrew Wright

Astonomy undergraduate at UMass Amherst. Currently I am doing research in high energy astrophysics under Prof. Daniel Wang. I am studying the potential doppler shift and broadening of the supermassive black hole Sgr A* that would be seen by future NASA telescopes, Lynx and Xrism. By using a 3D hydrodynamic simulation and SOXS coding software I can determine the velocity and direction of the surrounding gas which will help determine the innate properties of the black hole such as spin and why it is so quiet in X-ray emissions.
